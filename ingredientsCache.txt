## **NOTES ABOUT FOOD CHOICES**

**Seasonal Appropriateness (Winter):**
- Sweet potatoes, spinach, and stored grains are excellent winter choices
- Salmon is available year-round
- Bananas and avocados are imported but consistently available

**Cost Effectiveness (80/20 rule):**
- **Budget-friendly (80%)**: Chicken breast, eggs, oats, brown rice, sweet potatoes, spinach, milk, bananas
- **Premium items (20%)**: Atlantic salmon, almonds, avocados, extra virgin olive oil

**Versatility:**
- **Breakfast**: Oats with banana and almonds, eggs with spinach
- **Lunch/Dinner**: Chicken with sweet potato and spinach, salmon with rice and avocado
- **Snacks**: Almonds, banana with almond butter (made from almonds)

**Meal Distribution:**
- Proteins can easily be distributed across all meals
- Carbs provide energy for different times of day
- Healthy fats from multiple sources support hormone production and satiety

**Storage & Prep:**
- Most items have good shelf life
- Sweet potatoes and bananas ripen at different rates for extended use
- Frozen spinach could be substituted if fresh spoils too quickly

This list provides a solid nutritional foundation while remaining practical, affordable, and seasonally appropriate for winter shopping.
